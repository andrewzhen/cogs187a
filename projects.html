Quickly

Web Application Designed for College Students

Quickly is a tool that enables students to download their class schedules into their calendars instead of having to look it up on their school websites or having to manually enter class start and end times, meetings each week, section id, and professor.

The tool works by converting data that is pulled from the school system on a per-quarter basis into a calendar (.ics) file. Rest assured, the logistical details are accurate for every class successfully added since the range of possible classes are condensed each time any option is selected in the dropdown menu.

Now, there's no excuse for not having a calendar to share with your friends and family.

my Money

Finance Tracker Application

my Money is a finance tracker for all your expenses and assets throughout the year. Simply tell the tool basic details such as the date of purchase, a brief description of it, the cost and finally, the category type.

Users have the ability to view based on academic year (e.g. 2018-19), view all expenses and assets for that year, add a new purchase, update a date, description, value or type, and delete any existing purchase. Lastly, there is a helpful summary that tells you exactly how much you spent on each category, each academic quarter (including breaks), each month of the year, and how much total.
